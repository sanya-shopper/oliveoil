# Research notes: Government small-business / industry data & help
Compiled 2026-07-23. Live-checked (browser UA). Only 200-confirmed URLs made clickable on the site.

## Honest framing
No US government dataset counts "olive-oil businesses" as a distinct population — too niche; folded into broad NAICS. Use broad-code counts as an UPPER BOUND. USDA has olive-oil-specific data only on the ag/PRODUCTION side (acreage, tonnage, oil-vs-canning share), not business counts/revenue.

## NAICS codes olive oil hides inside (2022 NAICS, titles verified)
- 311225 Fats and Oils Refining and Blending (closest to bottling/blending bulk oil)
- 311999 All Other Miscellaneous Food Manufacturing
- 424490 Other Grocery and Related Products Merchant Wholesalers (defn includes bottling + wholesaling others' products)
- 445298 All Other Specialty Food Retailers (olive-oil retail shop). Pre-2022 = 445299 All Other Specialty Food Stores.
- CORRECTION: request had "445250" which is Fish and Seafood Retailers — NOT relevant. Use 445298.

# Research notes: Market Prices & Getting Quotes
Compiled 2026-07-23 by research agent. Prices are dated snapshots — highly volatile.

## Prices: spike then correction
- 2023-24 record spike (Mediterranean drought; Spain ~40% of world output). Global bulk EVOO peaked ~US$10,281/MT Jan 2024 (~$10.28/kg). Spain wholesale ~€9,000/tonne Jan 2024. Italy >€9/kg through 2024.
- 2024/25 EU output rebounded ~1.9-2.0M MT (Spain +~48% to ~1.26M t). Global EVOO fell ~32% from peak. Spain wholesale ~€4,180/t by Sep 2025; ~€3.60/kg at origin late June 2025.
- Jan 2026 bulk by origin (Certified Origins): Spain €4.50-5.00/kg (organic ~€5.50); Italy ~€6.90/kg (organic ~€7.40); Tunisia ~€3.40/kg; Greece (Chania IOC) ~€4.30/kg.
- IOC 2026 weekly: Jaén ~€3.79/kg (+7.5% YoY); Bari ~€5.65/kg (-41.8% YoY).
- US bulk: export unit value ~$3.97/kg (~$1.80/lb) +7.2% YoY (Selina Wamucii, export proxy). Premium CA sells higher. Small-lot CA retail bulk: $65-99/gal (~$17-26/L); Cobram $65/gal tin, Sonoma Farm $99/gal, 5-gal ~$355.
- Conversions: 1 MT = 1000 kg ≈ 1,087 L (density 0.916 kg/L); 1 kg ≈ 2.205 lb; 1 gal ≈ 3.785 L ≈ 3.47 kg.
- CAVEAT: OliveA warned May 2026 ~70% of 2025/26 crop sold in 7 months; prelim ~37% drop in 2026/27 flowering fertility => another price rise is a live risk.

## Retail benchmarks & cost stack (500ml)
- 500ml retail: $7-16 supermarket/mid-tier; $15-30+ premium 100% California/artisan.
- Bulk oil in a 500ml bottle: ~$1.90-2.50 (460g fill @ ~$4-5/kg).
- Illustrative cost stack: oil ~$2-3; glass bottle ~$0.50-1.20; cap/pour+band ~$0.15-0.40; label(s) ~$0.10-0.40; co-packing/fill/QA ~$0.50-1.50/unit. Landed COGS ~$4-7/bottle.
- Distributor margin ~25-30%; retailer markup ~40-50%+. $5-6 COGS -> ~$12-16 retail via 3-tier. DTC captures more.

## Getting a quote (RFQ)
Who: domestic mills (Cobram/COR, Corto, estates) for US-origin; importers/brokers (Jedwards/Bulk Natural Oils, Olive Oil Lovers, Centra Foods) for imported/blended.
Process: (1) RFQ w/ grade, volume, packaging (retail glass/tin vs IBC/flexitank/drum), destination, schedule, certs, Incoterms; (2) request samples (often 3x250ml, ~10-14 days); taste + lab; (3) negotiate price/MOQ/lead/terms; trial order before contract.
Certificate of Analysis specs to demand: harvest/crush date + best-by; free fatty acid <=0.8% (EVOO); peroxide value <=20 meq O2/kg; UV K232/K270; total polyphenols mg/kg; fatty acid + sterol profile (authenticity); certs (USDA NOP/EU Organic, Kosher, Halal, non-GMO, PDO/PGI).
MOQ: small distributors by case/few gal; true bulk ~20 MT = 1 flexitank; 100+ MT annual contracts. Incoterms: EXW/FOB/CIF/DDP (DDP simplest, priciest). Lead: samples 10-14 days; container from Med 4-10+ weeks. Payment: deposit + balance (e.g., 30/70), L/C large orders, net terms after relationship.

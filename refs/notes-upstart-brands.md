# Research notes: Upstart DTC olive oil brands (for comparison)
Compiled 2026-07-23. URLs verified in search/fetch. Funding/award claims sometimes self-reported.

## PROVENANCE (critical — represent honestly)
- US-GROWN (California): Brightland, Fat Gold, Wonder Valley, Nuvo.
- IMPORTED: Graza (Spain), Kosterina (Greece), Ellora Farms (Greece). Grove & Vine = rotating/mixed (CA + Spain/Greece/Tunisia).
- Do NOT call Graza or Kosterina American-grown — US-founded brands selling imported oil.

## Brands
- Graza — https://www.graza.co — bright-green plastic SQUEEZE bottles ("Drizzle" finish ~$20, "Sizzle" cook ~$15; 2025 "Frizzle" spray). Fresh single-source everyday-cooking EVOO, playful, cook-vs-finish. Founders Andrew Benin + Allen Dushi, founded 2021 / launched Jan 2022. Oil: IMPORTED Spain (Picual, Andalusia); HQ Brooklyn NY. ~$48.4M projected 2024 rev; ~$240M valuation; 28,000+ stores; ~5th-largest US brand (Aug 2025). Media: CNBC 2024-06-07 https://www.cnbc.com/2024/06/07/how-graza-olive-oil-co-founder-andrew-benin-built-the-business.html ; Forbes 2025-02-25 https://www.forbes.com/sites/andrewwatman/2025/02/25/drizzle-sizzle-and-now-frizzle-graza-debuts-new-high-heat-cooking-oil-and-spray-bottle-format/
- Brightland — https://brightland.co — matte/opaque GLASS bottles, art labels, 375ml ~$37-40; "AWAKE" (robust) + "ALIVE" (delicate); 2025 "Everyday" plastic squeeze at ~half price. Design-led everyday luxury, anti-rancidity education. Founder Aishwarya Iyer, 2018 (~$30k self-funded). Oil: US-GROWN California. ~$15M total raised incl Fall 2024 Series A; Oprah's Favorite Things. Media: Forbes https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbes-personal-shopper/article/brightland-aishwarya-iyer-/ ; Fast Company 2025 https://www.fastcompany.com/91358423/brightland-new-squeeze-bottles-everyday-oil ; Fortune 2025-07-01 https://dc.fortune.com/2025/07/01/brightland-olive-oil-everyday-luxury-series-a
- Fat Gold — https://fat.gold — 500ml metal TINS, subscription (4 tins/yr single-variety); no label, collectible MAGNET each tin. Micro-batch hyper-local CA craft, freshness/single-variety. Founders Kathryn Tomajan + Robin Sloan, 2016-2017. Oil: US-GROWN California (~3 acres Sunol/Central Valley). Bootstrapped. Media: Edible East Bay 2017-11-15 https://edibleeastbay.com/2017/11/15/fat-gold-olive-oil/ ; featured in Samin Nosrat "Good Things" + NYT/Wirecutter roundup (no single marquee feature — flag).
- Wonder Valley — https://welcometowondervalley.com — minimalist frosted/clear GLASS bottle + "Picnic" size; high-polyphenol CA EVOO; extends to olive-oil skincare. Wellness+culinary, desert aesthetic. Founders Alison Carroll (ex-marketing dir COOC) + Jay Carroll (ex-Levi's), 2014, Joshua Tree CA (town Wonder Valley, NOT Ojai). Oil: US-GROWN California. Independent. Media: Fashionista 2025-07 https://fashionista.com/2025/07/wonder-valley-olive-oil-beauty-wellness-brand ; Goop https://goop.com/wonder-valley-picnic-olive-oil/p/
- Kosterina — https://www.kosterina.com — dark/tinted GLASS bottles + tins; early-harvest high-polyphenol; 500ml ~$32; vinegars/olives/skincare. Wellness "superfood" Greek EVOO. Founder Katerina Mountanos, founded 2016 (side project), broad launch ~2019-2020. Oil: IMPORTED Greece (Koroneiki, Peloponnese). ~$500k pre-seed + ~$3.5M seed (investors Marc Lore, Andy Dunn). Media: Forbes 2021-11-02 https://www.forbes.com/sites/tanyaklich/2021/11/02/kosterina-spills-into-the-beauty-market-with-olive-oil-skincare-line/
- Grove and Vine — https://groveandvine.com — subscription single-origin small-batch, harvest dates + lab results, air-shipped. Founder Nick Coleman (certified taster). ~$195/yr (375ml). Oil: MIXED (CA + Spain/Greece/Tunisia rotating). Less-documented.
- Nuvo — https://nuvooliveoil.com — NYIOOC-winning CA EVOO, DTC, est 2012, H. Marin Farms Oroville CA. US-GROWN California. Producer/award brand more than VC upstart.
- Ellora Farms — https://ellorafarms.com — PDO Kolymvari Crete Greece; traditional heritage IMPORT, NOT a US design upstart — exclude or label clearly.

## Packaging archetypes for the site's format comparison (own SVG illustrations, not product photos)
- Squeeze bottle -> Graza (also Brightland Everyday)
- Tall designer glass (matte) -> Brightland Awake/Alive; frosted minimalist -> Wonder Valley
- Metal tin -> Fat Gold
- Dark/tinted glass -> Kosterina, traditional EVOO
- Refill fusti cask -> NY tasting-room shops (Saratoga Olive Oil Co, etc.)

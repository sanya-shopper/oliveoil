# Research notes: Bottling, Labeling & Regulations (NY)
Compiled 2026-07-23 by research agent. Raw material for the site; verify before publishing claims as advice.

## Federal (FDA/USDA)
- Bottling/packing olive oil => you are an FDA "food facility." Registration + biennial renewal are FREE via FDA Industry Systems. Renewal window: even years, Oct 1–Dec 31 (next: Oct–Dec 2026).
- Mandatory label elements (21 CFR 101): (1) statement of identity ("Extra Virgin Olive Oil") on front; (2) net quantity US + metric ("16 FL OZ (473 mL)"); (3) ingredient statement; (4) name & place of business; (5) Nutrition Facts panel.
- Small-business nutrition exemption 21 CFR 101.9(j): (j)(18) = <100 FTE employees AND <100,000 units/yr; requires annual notice to FDA; LOST if you make any nutrient-content/health claim.
- FSMA preventive controls (21 CFR 117): CGMPs apply; "qualified facility" (~<$1M/yr) exempt from full plan, files attestation. Confirm status.
- "Extra virgin": NO mandatory US grade. USDA AMS voluntary grades (US Extra Virgin = median defect 0, fruitiness >0, FFA <=0.8). FDA enforces against misleading labels.

## New York State
- Article 20-C Food Processing Establishment license REQUIRED to bottle/pack for wholesale/retail. NYS Dept of Ag & Markets.
- Fees (FSI-303): $400 standard; $175 if <=10 FTE & not franchise; $900 high-oversight; $0 in state incubator. Biennial. ~60-day processing. Premises inspection. Mail to 10B Airline Drive, Albany NY 12235.
- NY Home Processing (cottage food) does NOT cover olive oil — ineligible list explicitly includes "Vegetable oils, blended oils, salad dressings." Must use licensed 20-C facility or co-packer.

## Packaging
- Dark glass or tins. Dark glass shelf life ~12–18 mo vs ~3–6 mo clear/plastic. Tins block light completely.
- Limit oxygen/headspace. Store cool/dark (~61–64°F). Peak quality ~18–24 mo from harvest; print harvest date.
- Sizes: 250/500/750 mL. Closures: screw, tamper-evident, pour spouts.
- Suppliers: SKS Bottle & Packaging (Saratoga Springs, NY — local!), Berlin Packaging (O.Berk now part of it), Specialty Bottle, Uline.
- Co-packers/private label: Cibaria International (no min), OlivaMed/Liquid Mfg Solutions (OH), California Olive Oil Co-Packer (no min). You still own FDA registration + label compliance.

## Steps checklist
1. Facility model: own 20-C facility OR co-packer (home kitchen NOT allowed for oil).
2. Register facility with FDA (free).
3. Get NY 20-C license (FSI-303; $175/$400/$900; ~60 days; inspection).
4. Verify grade claims; keep supplier spec on file.
5. Compliant label OR file 101.9(j) exemption + make no claims.
6. Assess FSMA qualified-facility status.
7. Packaging: dark glass/tin, size/closure, best-by from harvest.
8. Source bottles/labels; bottle, label, code-date, sell.

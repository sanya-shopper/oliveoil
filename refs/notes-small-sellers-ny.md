# Research notes: Small NY-connected olive oil sellers
Compiled 2026-07-23 by research agent. Confirmed via primary sources actually read.

## The NY reality (key teaching point)
NY grows essentially no olives (too cold). Any "NY olive oil" = curation & commerce, not agriculture. Confirmed businesses are almost all TASTING-ROOM RETAILERS: buy bulk EVOO + balsamic sourced globally, store in stainless fusti casks, sell refill-by-the-bottle after sampling. Dominant sourcing = "follow the crush" (N. Hemisphere winter/spring, S. Hemisphere Chile/Australia/S.Africa summer/fall) so shelf oil is near-fresh. Common wholesale supplier behind many shops: Veronica Foods (California). NO confirmed NY commercial olive grower/presser — cold-climate is hobby/greenhouse novelty. A would-be Upstate "olive oil startup" is entering the specialty-retail / importer-bottler trade, not farming.

## Confirmed, appear active (7)
- Saratoga Olive Oil Company — Saratoga Springs (484 Broadway flagship) + Lake Placid NY + Burlington VT. Fusti tasting bar + refill + big online/DTC (Amazon, national shipping). Family Braidwood (Chad, Barbara, Clint), founded 2011; founders ex-pharma research consultants pivoted to fresh-EVOO diet/lifestyle; "follow the crush." Strongest, most-expanded NY example. Role model. https://saratogaoliveoil.com/pages/about
- Oliva! Gourmet Olive Oils & Vinegars — Albany (Stuyvesant Plaza) + Rhinebeck NY + Lenox MA. Family tasting room; imports bulk direct to stores (no distributor), 50+ flavors, fusti, bottle-to-order. Capital Region + Hudson Valley. https://www.olivaevoo.com/
- F. Oliver's (aka EVO OH!) — Finger Lakes: Canandaigua (original), Rochester (Park Ave), Ithaca (The Commons); historically Skaneateles. Experiential tasting shop, refill; curates local pasta/spices. Founded 2010 Penelope Pankow (Cornell Hotel School MS); sources via Veronica Foods, some NY-local tied to Cornell/Geneva station. https://folivers.com/
- The Blue Olive — Pawling + Cold Spring NY (+ Poughkeepsie referenced). Family tasting bar, 50+ EVOOs, balsamics, pasta/salts/spices, olive-oil soaps, pottery. Founded July 2014 John Canevari, certified olive oil sommelier; does "Olive Oil 101" at libraries/schools. Dutchess/Putnam Hudson Valley. https://mainstreetmag.com/the-blue-olive/
- Scarborough Fare — Beacon NY (Hudson Valley). Tasting bar refill-at-the-tap + curated pantry (salts, spices, pasta, honey, tea, mozzarella, Italian meats). Founded 2011, mother-son team (Donna & Cory); oils "researched, tested, handpicked" worldwide. Close analog to the mom-and-pop vision. https://scarboroughfarebeacon.com/
- The House of Olives — Buffalo NY (Hertel Ave). Family EVOO/balsamic/salt/spice tasting shop. Opened by Sandy Hofschneider (ran olive oil business in Charlotte NC before Buffalo). Western NY market. https://www.thehouseofolives.com/
- Essex Olive & Spice — NYC Lower East Side (88 Essex St). Importer/specialty-retail storefront (+ wholesale), not fusti bar — imported EVOO, aged Modena balsamics, olives, spices. Contact Saad Bourkadi. NYC-metro importer flavor. Founding detail thin (lightly sourced). https://www.essexoliveandspice.com/

## Negative / myth-busting (useful framing)
- vomFASS = German "from the cask" franchise in US but NO NY location (CA/KY/WI/MO); "vomFASS Lincoln Square" is Chicago neighborhood, not NYC. Don't call it NY.
- "Olive Ur Way" / "The Olive Oil Company" — could NOT confirm a specific NY business; don't cite.
- SKS Bottle & Packaging (Watervliet/Saratoga area) = packaging/glass supplier, not a seller — a SUPPLIER to a startup, not competitor.
- No NY farm grows/presses olives commercially. Container/greenhouse novelty only. Core teaching: a NY olive oil brand is a curator/importer-bottler, not a grower.

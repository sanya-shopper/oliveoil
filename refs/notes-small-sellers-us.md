# Research notes: Small / family / mom-and-pop US olive oil sellers (nationwide)
Compiled 2026-07-23 by research agent. Award/superlative claims from company sites are self-reported — attribute as claims. Cost/margin ranges are from business-guide aggregators, illustrative not audited.

## Three replicable small-seller models
1. Grower-millers / agritourism estates — own grove+mill, estate oil premium + tours/tastings/bistro/events. Land+story = moat. "First/only mill in the state" hook works in AZ/TX/GA/OR.
2. Tasting-room oil-&-vinegar shops — NO grove. Buy oils/balsamics wholesale, dispense from fusti, taste-before-buy, sell refills + gourmet add-ons. Low capital, high margin, location-dependent. Most replicable. Scales to franchise (We Olive, Vom Fass).
3. DTC micro-brands / single-varietal — tiny volume, strong design+narrative, online/subscription. Competes on freshness/transparency/cachet.
Theme: highest margins from DTC (60-70% vs 25-35% wholesale) and charging for the EXPERIENCE (paid tastings/classes/tours).

## Growers, mills & agritourism
- Queen Creek Olive Mill (Queen Creek, AZ): ~100 acres, 7,000+ trees, AZ's only working mill. Marketplace, eatery, coffee roastery, "Olive Oil 101" tours. Textbook revenue-stacking + "only in state" destination. Open, medaling.
- Texas Hill Country Olive Co (Dripping Springs, TX): family (Gambini, founded 2008), ~17 acres, Tuscan mill house, tasting room, bistro, tours. Claims most-awarded in TX, 50+ medals (self-reported). Newer-region terroir credibility. Open.
- Durant Olive Mill / Red Ridge Farms (Dayton, OR): family estate, mill (2008) + winery + lavender + nursery on 135 acres; only "Olioteca" in PNW. Oil blended estate + sourced N. Cal. Diversified cool-climate agritourism. Open.
- Chaffin Family Orchards (Oroville, CA): century-old diversified farm; estate EVOO old-growth Mission + grass-fed meat, fruit, eggs, wool. Regenerative multi-product homestead; farm-direct/CSA. Open.
- Georgia Olive Farms (Lakeland, GA): grower coop, founded 2009; first commercial olive crop east of Mississippi (2011), first certified GA EVOO. SHD Arbequina/Arbosana/Koroneiki, field-to-mill <24h. "First = brand"; consulting sideline. Open/expanding. (Also Woodpecker Trail Olive Farm, Glennville GA — micro agritourism.)

## Tasting-room shops (most replicable)
Economics: fit-out/inventory $20K-50K (one 650 sq ft profitable shop cited); built-out 1,000 sq ft $100K-250K. Gross margin 30-50%+ DTC (60-70% no middlemen). Paid tastings/classes = fattest margin. Lift basket w/ balsamics, salts, soaps, tapenades, gift sets. Success: knowledgeable/cheerful staff, tourist/foodie location, lean inventory (don't launch 40 SKUs). (Sources: Olive Oil Source, FinModelsLab — aggregators, illustrative.)
- Saratoga Olive Oil Co (Saratoga Springs, NY): family (Braidwood). 50+ oils/balsamics/salts, taste + IN-STORE REFILLS (bring clean dry bottle). Sources post-harvest both hemispheres for freshness. No grove — pure copyable model. Multi-store + e-comm. Open.
- EVOO Marketplace (Littleton + Aspen, CO): family "sampling room" founded 2009, 50+ EVOOs + Modena balsamics, taste + nationwide shipping. Right-sizing lesson: consolidated 2023, closed Denver LoDo to focus Littleton + e-comm. Cautionary. Open.
- We Olive (CA-based franchise; CA/NV/TX + Brooklyn NY): franchised olive-oil-bar + wine-bar; ~40 CA EVOOs (COOC-certified) + wine, tapenades, etc. Turnkey franchise route; wine bar adds dwell/check. First East Coast store Boerum Hill Brooklyn. Open/franchising. (Verify store count live.)
- VomFASS (intl franchise, US stores incl SF): German 1994 refill-from-cask, 300+ locations; oils/vinegars/spirits/liqueurs, bring-back refills (~3.5M bottles/yr saved). Some US production Madison WI. Sustainability/refill brand + turnkey franchise. Open.

## DTC micro-brands
- Enzo's Table / Enzo Olive Oil (San Joaquin Valley, CA): family (Ricchiuti, 4th-gen), 100% organic estate, milled within hours; biscotti, vinegars, subscription. Awards engine: repeated NYIOOC golds + Olive Japan + BIOL; claims 200+ awards. Aspirational middle-ground (bigger than true mom-and-pop). Open/growing.
- Wonder Valley (Joshua Tree, CA): DTC micro-brand, founded 2014 (Alison & Jay Carroll; Alison ex-marketing dir COOC). Single hero product CA EVOO -> olive-oil skincare. Ran low-pressure retail "testing ground" ~4 yrs to learn customer before scaling. Design/story/wellness de-commodifies oil. Open/growing.
- Fat Gold (SF Bay Area, CA): micro subscription-first, launched 2016-17 (Kathryn Tomajan, COOC sensory panel; Robin Sloan). Annual sub = 4x 500ml tins/yr, single-variety batches, ~$179/yr; collectible magnets not labels. Clearest "de-commodify EVOO" model; recurring revenue, no storefront. Also select specialty grocers. Open.
- Bariani Olive Oil (Sacramento/Zamora, CA): family grower-miller + DTC since ~1991 (Lombardy immigrants). Hand-picked Manzanillo/Mission, stone-wheel milled <48h, hydraulic-pressed, unfiltered organic. Old-world method + terroir, quiet craftsman, 30+ yrs. Open.

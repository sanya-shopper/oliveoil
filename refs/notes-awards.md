# Research notes: Olive oil awards / competitions & winners
Compiled 2026-07-23. Producer award tallies are self/organizer-reported, not audited.

## Competitions
- NYIOOC World Olive Oil Competition — https://nyiooc.org/ (results https://bestoliveoils.org/). NYC, founded 2013. Largest quality contest: 2025 awarded 742 oils (517 Gold, 225 Silver) from 1,000+ entries; 2026 awarded 698 (464 Gold, 234 Silver) from 1,021 oils / 29 countries. US ~84 (2025), ~95 (2026). De facto global benchmark; heavy mainstream press. Most important for a small US producer.
- Olive Japan International — https://olivejapan.com/en/competition. Tokyo. Prestigious Asian-market comp; entry ~€300; 2026 results June 12. Good for Asian retail.
- EVO IOOC Italy (EVO International Olive Oil Contest) — https://www.evo-iooc.it/en/. Palmi, Calabria. 10th ed May 2025. IOC-method blind tasting. DISTINCT from EVOOLEUM.
- EVOOLEUM (separate, Spain) — https://www.evooleum.com/en/. Mercacei + AEMO; names world Top 100 EVOOs. ("Domina" alias = UNVERIFIED, don't use.)
- London International Olive Oil Competitions (LIOOC) — https://www.londonoliveoil.com/ (results https://globaloliveoilstars.com/). Quality + Health Claim + Design contests. Lower profile; brand-building. Mild caution on prestige claims.
- LA International Extra Virgin Olive Oil Competition (Fairplex) — https://fairplex.com/competitions/olive-oil-competition/ (results https://www.laoliveoilcomp.com/). Pomona CA, nonprofit, 27th ed 2026. 2026: 588 entries/209 producers; 2025: 493/200. Top US-based intl comp; accessible/credible for CA/US small producers.
- COOC Seal Certification — https://cooc.com/ (certified oils https://cooc.com/certified-oils/). Not a ranked award — CERTIFICATION requiring chem + sensory; stricter than intl norms. Trust mark for genuine CA EVOO.
- Yolo County Fair Olive Oil Competition — Woodland CA; "2nd-largest US comp" (flag counts). California State Fair EVOO — https://castatefair.com/competitions/ (CA-only).

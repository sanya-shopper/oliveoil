How to use this

Bookmark the ones relevant to your next step. The three you'll return to most: the International Olive Council and PoolRed for live prices, NYS Ag & Markets for the 20-C license, and FDA Food for registration and labeling. Everything below was accessed 2026-07-23; agencies update their pages, so if a link has moved, search the site for the document title.

Method & caveats

This guide was assembled from public web sources over a single research pass in July 2026. A few honest limitations to keep in mind:

  • Prices are snapshots. Olive oil is a volatile commodity — figures here will drift. Always confirm against a live tracker and a real quote.
  • Market sizes are estimates. Research firms disagree; treat any single number as directional, not exact.
  • Some claims are flagged. Where a figure came from a secondary aggregator or a single source (e.g. certain revenue/share estimates, one product-quality allegation), the text says so.
  • Nothing here is legal, financial, or business advice. Regulations especially must be confirmed with the FDA and NYS Department of Agriculture & Markets before you act.
